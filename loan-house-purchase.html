What is the minimum down payment required?

+

Can I get tax benefits on home loans?

+

Yes, you can claim tax deductions under Section 80C (up to ‚Çπ1.5 lakhs for principal repayment) and Section 24(b) (up to ‚Çπ2 lakhs for interest payment) under the Income Tax Act.

What happens if I want to prepay my loan?

+

For floating rate home loans, there are usually no prepayment charges. For fixed rate loans, banks may charge 2-3% of the outstanding amount as prepayment penalty.

Is home loan insurance mandatory?

+

Home loan insurance is not mandatory but highly recommended. It protects your family from the loan burden in case of unforeseen circumstances. Property insurance is usually mandatory.
