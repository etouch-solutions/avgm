Land Loan Process Steps

1. Land Selection & Verification

Choose your plot and verify all legal documents including title deed, survey settlement, and land use permissions. Our experts assist in complete due diligence.

  • Site visit and inspection
  • Legal document verification
  • Survey settlement check
  • Land use permission validation

2. Loan Application Submission

Submit your loan application with required documents. Our team ensures complete documentation for faster processing.

  • Application form completion
  • Income and identity proofs
  • Property documents submission
  • Initial eligibility assessment

3. Technical & Legal Evaluation

Bank conducts technical valuation of the land and legal verification of all submitted documents for final approval.

  • Property valuation
  • Legal verification
  • Credit score assessment
  • Approval committee review

4. Loan Sanction & Disbursement

Upon approval, loan amount is sanctioned and disbursed either directly to seller or through escrow account.

  • Loan sanction letter
  • Registration completion
  • Fund disbursement
  • Post-disbursement formalities
